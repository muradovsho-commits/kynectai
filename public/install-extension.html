Chrome Extension

OfferBell for Gmail

Add contacts from Gmail to your Outreach Tracker with one click. Takes 30 seconds to set up.

Download Extension

Setup in 4 steps

1

Download & Unzip

Click the button above. Unzip the downloaded file. Inside you'll see a folder called chrome-extension — that's the one you need.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

In Chrome, go to chrome://extensions in your address bar. Toggle on Developer mode in the top-right corner.

3

Load the extension

Click Load unpacked in the top-left. Navigate to and select the chrome-extension folder you unzipped.

4

Open Gmail

Go to Gmail and open any email. You'll see the purple Add to OfferBell button in the toolbar. Click it to save contacts to your tracker.

Note: Chrome may show a warning about developer mode extensions. This is normal for self-hosted extensions. Click "Keep" to continue. We're working on getting OfferBell listed on the Chrome Web Store for one-click install.
